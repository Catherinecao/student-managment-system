The purpose of this application is to get from you the information we need to offer you a place and to enrol you onto a course at KIBT. Prior to completing this form, all students must first read the KIBT Student Handbook in full. This is available on request from KIBT or can be viewed at www.kingston.ac.nz. Prospective students must also review the Code of Practice for the Pastoral care of International Students published by the New Zealand Qualification Authority (NZQA).
